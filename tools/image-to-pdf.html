Frequently asked questions

Can I combine multiple images into one PDF?

Yes. Add as many JPG, PNG, or WEBP images as you like, reorder them, and they'll all be combined into a single multi-page PDF.

Are my images uploaded anywhere?

No. This tool builds the PDF entirely in your browser using JavaScript, so your images never leave your device.

Can I control the page size and orientation?

Yes. Choose between A4, Letter, or fit-to-image sizing, and portrait or landscape orientation, before exporting.

Does this tool add a watermark to my PDF?

No. The PDF you download is clean, with no watermark or branding added.
