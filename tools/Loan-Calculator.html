Free Loan Calculator Online

This Loan Calculator is a powerful online tool that helps you calculate your monthly EMI, total payment, and total interest instantly. Whether you are planning a home loan, personal loan, or car loan, this tool gives accurate results within seconds.

How to Use Loan Calculator

Enter your loan amount, interest rate, and loan term in years. Click calculate and instantly see your monthly EMI breakdown, total payment, and full amortization schedule. Our online loan calculator automatically calculates everything.

Why Use This EMI Calculator?

This EMI calculator is fast, accurate, and easy to use. It works on all devices and helps you plan your finances better. Features include donut chart, rate comparison, amortization table, extra payment savings, and WhatsApp sharing.

Understanding EMI

EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is the fixed monthly payment you make to repay a loan. It includes both principal and interest. Use our calculator to understand exactly how your money is split between principal and interest each month.
