Frequently asked questions

Can I resize an image to an exact pixel size?

Yes. Enter an exact width and height in pixels, and optionally lock the aspect ratio so the image doesn't stretch or distort.

Are there presets for social media?

Yes. Presets are included for Instagram post, Instagram story, Facebook cover, YouTube thumbnail, and Twitter/X post, so you don't need to look up exact dimensions.

Are my images uploaded to a server?

No. Resizing happens locally in your browser using the Canvas API, so your images never leave your device.

Will resizing make my image blurry?

Enlarging an image beyond its original resolution can make it look softer, since no new detail is created. Reducing the size generally stays sharp.
