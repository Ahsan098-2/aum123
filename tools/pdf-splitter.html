Frequently Asked Questions

Is this PDF splitter really free?+
Yes — 100% free with no usage limits, no subscriptions, and no hidden charges. Split as many PDFs as you need.
Does my PDF get uploaded to a server?+
No. Everything runs entirely in your browser using pdf-lib.js. Your PDF files are never sent to any server. They stay on your device the whole time.
How do I separate PDF pages online?+
Upload your PDF, select a split mode (Range, Custom, Each Page, etc.), fill in the page numbers, and click Split. Your output PDFs download immediately.
What is the maximum PDF size supported?+
Up to 100MB per file. For very large PDFs with hundreds of pages, processing may take a few seconds depending on your device speed.
Can I extract specific non-consecutive pages?+
Yes! Use Custom Pages mode and enter something like 1,3,5-8,12 to extract pages 1, 3, 5 through 8, and 12 into a single PDF.
What does "Split Each Page" do?+
It creates a separate PDF file for every single page of your document. A 10-page PDF becomes 10 individual single-page PDF files.
How do I split a PDF into equal parts?+
Use Fixed Chunks mode. Enter the number of pages per chunk (e.g. 5) and the tool will automatically split your PDF into equal parts of that size.
Can I split PDF on my phone?+
Yes! The tool is fully responsive and works on smartphones and tablets. Open it in your mobile browser and split PDFs just like on desktop.
