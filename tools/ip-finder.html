FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about checking your IP address and location online.

What is my IP address?+
Your IP address is the unique identifier your internet provider assigns your connection. This tool detects and displays it automatically the moment you open the page.
Is this IP lookup tool free with no registration?+
Yes, this is a 100% free IP address finder with no signup, no registration, and no hidden limits.
How accurate is the location shown for my IP?+
IP-based location is approximate and usually accurate to the city or region level, not your exact address. It is based on your ISP's registered location data.
Does this tool store my IP address?+
No. Your IP lookup happens directly between your browser and the lookup service. Daily Toolkit does not log or store your IP address.
Why is my IP location showing a different city?+
If you are using a VPN, proxy, or mobile data, your displayed location reflects your provider's server location rather than your physical location.
What is the difference between public IP and local IP?+
Your public IP is what websites see and is assigned by your internet provider. Your local IP is used only within your home or office network and is not visible to external websites.
