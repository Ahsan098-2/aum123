Frequently asked questions

Is this PDF to image converter really free?

Yes. There's no limit on conversions, no watermark on the output images, and no account is required.

Are my PDF files uploaded to a server?

No. This tool runs entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Your PDF is never uploaded anywhere, which makes it safe for private or confidential documents.

What image formats can I export to?

You can export each page as a JPG or a PNG. JPG gives a smaller file size, while PNG keeps a lossless, sharper result — useful for text-heavy pages.

Can I convert only some pages of my PDF?

Yes. After the PDF loads, every page shows up as its own thumbnail with a download button, so you can grab just the pages you need, or all of them as a ZIP.

Is there a page or file size limit?

There's no artificial limit. Very large PDFs may simply take longer since everything is processed on your own device.
