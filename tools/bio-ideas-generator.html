Tips for the Perfect Bio
Keep it under 150 characters for Instagram — shorter bios get more reads
Add a call-to-action — tell visitors what to do next (link in bio, DM me, etc.)
Use line breaks to make your bio easy to scan and visually appealing
Include relevant keywords that reflect your niche for better discoverability
Emojis add personality but don't overdo it — 3–5 emojis max works best
Update your bio regularly to reflect what you're currently working on

Frequently Asked Questions
What is a bio generator?+
A bio generator is an online tool that creates unique social media bio ideas based on your name, profession, hobbies and personality. It saves time and helps you write a bio that stands out.
Is this bio generator free?+
Yes, completely free. No signup, no subscription, no hidden fees. Generate as many bios as you like anytime.
Which platforms does this bio generator support?+
This tool supports Instagram, TikTok, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, YouTube, Snapchat and Facebook. Each platform has bio length and style recommendations built in.
How many characters can an Instagram bio have?+
Instagram allows up to 150 characters in your bio. Our generator keeps this limit in mind and shows a character count for every bio it creates.
Can I save my favourite bios?+
Yes! Click the Save button on any generated bio to add it to your Saved Bios list. Saved bios are stored in your browser so they stay even if you refresh the page.
What bio styles are available?+
We offer 8 styles: Aesthetic (dreamy & soft), Funny (humour & wit), Attitude (bold & confident), Professional (career-focused), Love (romantic & warm), Motivational (inspiring & energetic), Minimal (clean & simple), and Coder (tech & developer bios).
