Guide

How Unit Conversion Works

Why standard units matter

Most of the world uses the metric system, while a few countries — including the United States — primarily use imperial units. This converter bridges that gap, letting you move between systems instantly for cooking, travel, fitness, engineering, or everyday calculations.

Linear vs. non-linear conversions

Length, mass, area, volume, speed, and data storage all use simple multiplication because they share a common zero point:

General formula: Target value = Source value × Conversion factor

Temperature is different. Celsius, Fahrenheit, and Kelvin don't share a common zero, so conversions require offset-based formulas:

°F = (°C × 9/5) + 32
°C = (°F − 32) × 5/9
K = °C + 273.15

Precision and rounding

This tool calculates results to four decimal places for accuracy, which is more than sufficient for everyday use, cooking measurements, DIY projects, fitness tracking, and most academic or professional needs.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about using this unit converter.

What unit categories can I convert?+
This tool converts length, mass (weight), temperature, area, volume, speed, and digital storage units, covering the most commonly needed everyday and professional conversions.
Is this unit converter accurate?+
Yes. All conversions use standard internationally recognized conversion factors and are calculated instantly in your browser with high decimal precision.
Why is temperature different from other units?+
Unlike length or mass, temperature scales don't share a common zero point, so converting between Celsius, Fahrenheit, and Kelvin requires offset formulas rather than simple multiplication.
Can I convert length to weight?+
No. Unit conversion only works within the same category — meters can convert to feet, but not to kilograms, since they measure fundamentally different physical properties.
Does this tool store or upload my data?+
No. All calculations run locally in your browser using JavaScript. Nothing you type is sent to a server or saved.
Can I use this on mobile?+
Yes, the converter is fully responsive and works smoothly on phones, tablets, and desktops.
